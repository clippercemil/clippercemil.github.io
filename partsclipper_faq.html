Frequently Asked Questions


 What is PartsClipper?

Leveraging our manufacturing network in Turkey, we offer the best quality, price and speed-to-delivery combination in the market from prototyping to serial manufacturing of parts.

 What are PartsClipper’s services?

We are capable of many widely used manufacturing methods, namely CNC Machining, Sheet Forming, Welding, 3D Printing and Gear Production, as well as post-manufacturing processes like Surface Finishing. Our services also include assembly for specific needs.

 Does PartsClipper manufacture the parts?

No, we don’t have any machines of our own, but we do have a large manufacturing network in Turkey. We enable our customers to access the available capacity of our qualified and audited workshops within our manufacturing network while offering full visibility over the process.

 Which sectors do you serve?

We provide manufacturing for all industries that require metal and plastic parts. These sectors include defense, automotive, research, robotics, household appliances, machinery, aerospace and aviation, infrastructure and many more.

 Which materials do you work with?

We work with a wide variety of common metals and plastics as well as rare materials.

 Does PartsClipper have a Minimum Order of Quantity Policy?

We do not have a MoQ policy, because we know how delicate some processes can be, especially when it comes to prototyping. We accommodate our clients’ needs from prototyping to serial manufacturing.

 How does it work? What do I need to get a quote?

Not much! We create our quote based on all popular CAD files (STEP | STP | SLDPRT | IPT | 3DXML | PRT | SAT). You can also submit a PDF or another type of technical drawing with us for a quote. Just share your design and let us come back to you with the best quote within 24 hours.

 When will my quote be ready?

Typically, we send our quote within one business day on an EXW Turkey basis. For complex requests, it might take a bit more.

 How does PartsClipper achieve the best price - quality - speed combination?

Our manufacturing hub consists of vetted workshops that have years of experience in their relevant sectors. They have skilled workers and cutting-edge machine parks in addition to professional work. We are able to provide the best price - quality - speed combination in the market by tapping into their unused manufacturing capacity.

 How does PartsClipper assure quality?

We grow our hub based on certain criteria, manufacturing quality being the most important one. We work with seasoned manufacturers who have ISO9001 at a minimum and other certifications for various industrial standards. After the parts are manufactured, we perform internal quality control before every delivery. For specific quality control procedures, please contact our team.

 How does PartsClipper protect my intellectual property?

We are meticulous about our customers’ intellectual property. Your design files are anonymised and shared only with the specific partner who will manufacture the parts, within legal frames.

 What are my payment options?

At the moment, we accept SWIFT payments only.
