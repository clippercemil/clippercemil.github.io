CNC (Computer Numerical Control)


#CNC (Computer Numerical Control) #manufacturing is a method of production that uses computer-controlled machines to create precise and accurate products.


CNC (Computer Numerical Control)

The process involves the use of specialized software to control the movement of cutting tools and machinery, allowing for highly precise and consistent results. This type of manufacturing is particularly useful for creating complex parts or products that require a high degree of accuracy and repeatability. CNC machines are commonly used in industries such as #aerospace, #automotive, #medical and equipment manufacturing, as well as in #prototyping and small-scale production. The precision of CNC manufacturing is mainly due to the fact that it uses digital instructions to control the movement of the tools, thus eliminating human error and enabling high precision and repeatability in the production process.
